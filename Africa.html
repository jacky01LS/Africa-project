This year I was lucky enough to travel to the beautiful continent of Africa. I started out in Johannesburg of South Africa, and made my way over to Zimbabwe and Botswana! This trip was focused towards the incredible wildlife and nature reserves, and Victoria Falls - one of the seven natural wonders of the world. The 'Big Five' of Africa are the Leopard, Lion, Elephant, Rhino, and Buffalo... I was fortunately able to encounter all five, and so much more.
